Bring home The Super Mario Bros. Movie


The Power Up Edition is now available for pre-purchase at the My Nintendo™ Store for fans who’d like to experience The Super Mario Bros. Movie at home!
The movie tells the story of two Brooklyn plumbers who are brothers and best friends: Mario, the brave one with the "let's-a go" attitude, and the perpetually anxious Luigi, who would prefer to go nowhere. We open with the Super Mario Brothers reckoning with their struggling plumbing business, and wind up in a whirlwind adventure through Mushroom Kingdom.
The Power Up Edition includes these special features:

  • Getting to Know the Cast
  • Leveling Up: Making The Super Mario Bros. Movie
  • The Super Mario Bros. Movie Field Guide
  • “Peaches” Lyric Video
  • Leadership Lessons from Anya Taylor-Joy
