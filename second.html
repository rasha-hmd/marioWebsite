Pack your kart for the fun-filled Vacation Tour!


It’s time to have a blast of fun and sun with the Vacation Tour, the latest limited-time event in the Mario Kart Tour game, happening now!
Visit exciting city destinations like Paris, Athens, Bangkok, and take in the sights of lovely locations around the Mushroom Kingdom in fun-filled races. Plus, you can take a lemony-fresh plunge into newly added course Squeaky Clean Sprint.
Kick back in the new Goo-Goo Gold, Dolphin Drifter, and Purple Rattle Buggy karts; and shade yourself with the newly added Dolphin Great Sail and Pink Bubble Balloon gliders. You can also dress up your Mii™ character with the new Wendy Mii Racing Suit.
The Vacation Tour runs from now until August 22, 2023 at 10:59 p.m. PT. We hope you have fun!

Download & dive in Two ways to race on Squeaky Clean Sprint

The Squeaky Clean Sprint course featured in the Vacation Tour is also available in the Mario Kart™ 8 Deluxe game on the Nintendo Switch™ system! The course is part of the paid Mario Kart 8 Deluxe – Booster Course Pass* DLC, available now from Nintendo.com and Nintendo eShop on your device.
Plus, racers who have an active Nintendo Switch Online + Expansion Pack** membership have access to the Mario Kart 8 Deluxe – Booster Course Pass DLC at no additional cost.
