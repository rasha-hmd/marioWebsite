The classic Mario RPG returns with an updated look! Can you save Star Road?


The nefarious Smithy Gang has shattered Star Road and no new wishes can be made! Luckily, a group of oddball heroes are here to save the day—Mario, Bowser, Peach, and original characters Mallow and Geno. Err, yes—oddball indeed!
